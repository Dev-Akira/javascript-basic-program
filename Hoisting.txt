<h2>JavaScript Hoisting</h2>

<p>With <b>var</b>, you can use a variable before it is declared:</p>

<p id="demo"></p>

<script>
carName = "Volvo";
document.getElementById("demo").innerHTML = carName;
var carName;
</script>
